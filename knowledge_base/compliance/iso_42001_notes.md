# ISO/IEC 42001 Notes (Anonymized)

This is a lightweight reminder document intended as a knowledge base source, not legal advice.

## Common AI Management System Patterns
- Define AI governance roles, approval paths, and decision-making accountability.
- Maintain documented objectives, policies, and review processes for responsible AI use.
- Keep an inventory of AI systems, providers, intended purpose, risks, and operating constraints.
- Require documented risk assessment, monitoring, incident response, and continual improvement activities for AI-enabled capabilities.
- Link AI controls to evidence such as model cards, evaluation records, change logs, oversight procedures, and training records.
