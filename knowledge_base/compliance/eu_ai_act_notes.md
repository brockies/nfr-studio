# EU AI Act Notes (Anonymized)

This is a lightweight reminder document intended as a knowledge base source, not legal advice.

## Common AI Governance Patterns
- Classify the AI use case early and document whether prohibited, transparency, GPAI, or high-risk themes may apply.
- Maintain an inventory of AI systems, model providers, intended use, human oversight, and fallback mechanisms.
- Define AI literacy expectations for staff using, configuring, reviewing, or approving AI-assisted workflows.
- Record provider due diligence, model change management, monitoring, incident handling, and user-facing transparency obligations where relevant.
- For higher-risk use cases, plan evidence for testing, risk assessment, oversight, and post-deployment monitoring.
