# NIST AI RMF Notes (Anonymized)

This is a lightweight reminder document intended as a knowledge base source, not legal advice.

## Common AI Risk Management Patterns
- Establish governance for trustworthy AI across the lifecycle, including ownership, escalation, and review.
- Map intended use, affected stakeholders, failure modes, and misuse risks before production rollout.
- Measure model quality and socio-technical risks using repeatable evaluation criteria, monitoring, and documented thresholds.
- Manage ongoing changes through incident handling, provider review, retraining or prompt-change controls, and periodic reassessment.
- Preserve evidence showing how AI risks were governed, mapped, measured, and managed over time.
